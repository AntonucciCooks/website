How It Works

Whether you're looking for help getting healthy meals on the table during a busy week or planning a special gathering with family and friends, my goal is the same: to take the stress out of cooking so you can spend more time enjoying good food and the people around your table.

Weekly Personal Chef Services

Hiring a personal chef should make your life easier, not more complicated. I'll take care of the meal planning, grocery shopping, cooking, and cleanup so you can enjoy delicious, home-cooked meals throughout the week.

Step 1

Let's Get to Know Each Other

Every new client begins with a complimentary 30-minute consultation. We'll talk about your favorite foods, dietary needs, health goals, allergies, household size, and the types of meals that work best for your lifestyle.

Whether you're looking for family-friendly dinners, higher-protein meals, GLP-1 support, gluten-free options, or simply help getting dinner on the table, I'll create a plan tailored specifically to you.

Step 2

Menu Planning & Approval

Each week, I'll send a customized menu for your review and approval at least 48 hours before your scheduled cook day.

Need a few adjustments? No problem. My goal is to create meals you're genuinely excited to eat.

Step 3

Grocery Shopping

On the morning of your cook day, I'll shop for fresh, high-quality ingredients based on your approved menu. Grocery costs are billed separately at cost, with no markup.

Step 4

In-Home Meal Preparation

Using your kitchen, I'll prepare your meals for the week, package and label them, and provide reheating instructions when needed.

You do not need to be home during your service, although many clients choose to be. Either way, you'll return to a refrigerator stocked with meals that are ready when you are.

Step 5

Kitchen Cleanup

Before I leave, your kitchen will be cleaned and returned to the condition I found it in. The only evidence I've been there should be the delicious meals waiting in your refrigerator.

Dietary Needs & Preferences

Every menu is customized to your tastes, preferences, and goals. I regularly accommodate:

  • GLP-1 friendly meal planning
  • Bariatric nutrition needs
  • Higher-protein meal plans
  • Gluten-free diets
  • Food allergies and sensitivities
  • Low-FODMAP diets
  • Vegetarian and plant-forward meals

My philosophy is simple: food should support your goals without sacrificing flavor, enjoyment, or variety.

Pricing

Weekly Personal Chef Service starts at $350 per visit, plus the cost of groceries.

Pricing varies based on household size, number of meals requested, dietary needs, and service frequency.

Most clients choose a recurring weekly or bi-weekly schedule, but occasional services are also available.

Hosting a gathering should be fun. Whether you're celebrating a milestone birthday, hosting a dinner party, planning a baby shower, or gathering friends for brunch, I'll help create a memorable experience while you focus on your guests.
