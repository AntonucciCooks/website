Welcome to Antonucci Cooks!

I'm Mary Antonucci, a personal chef and board-certified nutrition coach based in Baltimore. I help busy people eat well without spending their evenings meal planning, grocery shopping, cooking, and cleaning.

Here's how it works: I shop for the ingredients, prepare meals tailored specifically to you in your own home, stock your fridge, and leave your kitchen just as I found it. When dinner rolls around, all that's left for you to do is enjoy it.

My clients include busy professionals, families, and people navigating health goals or dietary needs. I also have a special interest in supporting clients taking GLP-1 medications. My background in both cooking and nutrition allows me to create meals that are not only nutritious, but also satisfying, flavorful, and realistic for everyday life.

No two clients are alike, and I think your meals should reflect that. Before I ever step into your kitchen, I take the time to get to know you, your goals, your favorite foods, the meals that bring you comfort, and the ingredients you'd rather never see again. The result is a menu built around your life and your tastes.

Good food should not feel complicated. It should make your life easier, bring you joy, and give you one less thing to think about at the end of a long day.

Serving clients throughout Baltimore and surrounding communities.
